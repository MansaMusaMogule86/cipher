# Marketing Team Roles, Strategy, and Tasks

## Roles
- **Researcher**: Gathers market intelligence and insights
- **Writer**: Crafts high-converting marketing assets
- **Critic**: Identifies risks and challenges, provides critical feedback

## Strategy
- Leverage CIPHER's unique value: 88% creator payouts, anonymous fan codes, crypto payments
- Target creators frustrated with high fees and payout delays on competitors
- Use data-driven insights to inform campaigns and outreach

## Tasks
- Develop and test agent prompts for each role
- Integrate agent outputs into the marketing dashboard
- Launch multi-channel campaigns (Twitter, TikTok, Email)
- Monitor performance and iterate based on analytics and feedback
